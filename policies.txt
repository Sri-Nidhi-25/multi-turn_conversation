VACATION POLICY
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid vacation per year. 
Unused days can be carried over to the next year, but expire after 12 months.
No blackout dates apply, but approval from a manager is required.

REMOTE WORK POLICY
Remote work is allowed two days per week.
The company provides a laptop and a monthly internet stipend.
Equipment must be returned upon termination.

DRESS CODE
Business casual attire is required on office days.
Remote employees are expected to be presentable during video calls.

PROBATION PERIOD
New hires have a 90‑day probation period.
During this time, performance reviews occur at 30, 60, and 90 days.

LEAVE REQUEST PROCESS
Time off requests must be submitted via the HR system at least two weeks in advance.
For emergencies, contact your manager directly.
